Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Health Hub is a personal health and fitness tracking application. This policy explains how data from connected services is handled.

Data Collected

When recovery, nutrition, or health integrations are connected, Health Hub may retrieve:

  • Recovery scores, HRV, and resting heart rate
  • Sleep duration and sleep quality metrics
  • Nutrition, weight, steps, cardio, and workout logs

How Data Is Used

All data is used solely for personal cut execution, training, nutrition, and recovery tracking within this application. It is stored in a private Supabase database accessible only to the account owner.

Data Sharing

No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party. This application is for personal use only.

Data Retention

Data is retained until you choose to delete it. Disconnecting an integration stops future data collection from that service.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact the application owner directly.
