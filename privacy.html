Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Health Hub is a personal health and fitness tracking application. This policy explains how data from connected services (including Whoop) is handled.

Data Collected

When you connect your Whoop account, Health Hub retrieves:

  • Recovery scores, HRV, and resting heart rate
  • Sleep duration and sleep stage breakdowns
  • Daily strain scores

How Data Is Used

All data is used solely for personal health tracking within this application. It is stored in a private Supabase database accessible only to the account owner.

Data Sharing

No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party. This application is for personal use only.

Data Retention

Data is retained until you choose to delete it. You can disconnect your Whoop account at any time via the Whoop app or developer portal, which will stop future data collection.

Contact

For any questions about this policy, contact the application owner directly.
